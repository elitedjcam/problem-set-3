Mechanics

Game Modes and Difficulties

Minecraft as of today has two game modes; Survival and Creative. Creative gives players unlimited materials and flight, allowing them to do whatever they want with the world they're in. Survival is the meat of this discussion.

There are also five difficulty modes:

  1. Peaceful
  2. Easy
  3. Normal
  4. Hard
  5. Hardcore
  6. Adventure

The difficulties are ordered from easiest to the most difficult. Peaceful mode is the easiest due to the complete erasure of hunger depletion and mobs. Easy to Hard mode has mobs and hunger, but as difficulty increases, the amount of damage mobs do to you will be increased. Hardcore is all of this, but without natural health regeneration and the highest amount of damage that mobs deal to you.

Basics

Movement

Minecraft is awfully simple, since everything is tied to whatever keys you bind them to. You can move, strafe, jump, sneak, fight, mine, and even fish with whatever keys you use. But that's nearly every game. So what makes this game unique?

Crafting

Minecraft was one of the first few games that utilized a manual crafting model. You place materials into a specific pattern and out comes the crafted item. Update 1.12/1.13 came with an in-game crafting guide, but before that, you had to figure out for yourself. You can craft a variety of tools, armor, weapons (no, not guns, don't ask), and a good amount of utility and vanity items.

Mining and Building
