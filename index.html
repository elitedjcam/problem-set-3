What is Minecraft?

Minecraft is a well-known voxel video game prized for its simple and well-designed gameplay. Since its inception, many people have picked up and played the game, some loving it, some absolutely hating it, and some making semi-decent content on YouTube. The game has given birth to countless memes, Minecraft song parodies and note block covers. Join me as we dive into the framework of one of the most popular games in history. From famous individuals to the various mechanics within the game, I present to you: Minecraft.
