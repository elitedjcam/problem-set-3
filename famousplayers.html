Who are Some Famous Minecraft-Related People?

There are a multitude of people famous for their involvement in Minecraft. Here is a few of them.

C418

Daniel Rosenfield, also known as C418, composed the soundtrack and much of the sound effects in Minecraft. His website is here.

Grandayy/grande1899

Popular Youtuber and meme expert Grandayy has a second channel titled grande1899. There, he has uploaded a multitude of minecraft noteblock covers. Basically he takes song and remakes them with note blocks entirely by ear. One of his more popular covers is on the song Africa by Toto (click here) Here is also a link to his meme channel and his note block channel.

JackSucksAtLife

JackSucksAtLife is a Minecraft "Let's Play" Youtuber who shares his tales managing his server "SkyCade" and playing Minecraft with his friends and fellow Youtubers. His channel can be found here.

There are many more Youtubers who came before these individuals such as:

  • SkyDoesMinecraft
  • Bodil40
  • TrueMU
  • BajanCanadian

...and many more. However, much of the Minecraft Youtube monoliths have since turned to other sources of Youtube content or gave up on Minecraft entirely. Some believe that the game is dead.
